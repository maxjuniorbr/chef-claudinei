Our Story

Passion Forged
Over Open Fire

Chef Claudinei brings the vibrant flavors of Brazil straight to your backyard, garden party, or corporate event. With years of experience in authentic Brazilian churrasco, artisan charcuterie boards, and full-service outdoor catering, every event becomes a celebration of fire, flavor, and family.

From slow-grilled picanha and tender skewers to stunning grazing tables overflowing with artisan cheeses, meats, and seasonal fruits — we bring the full Brazilian spirit to your table.
